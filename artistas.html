TOP 5 THE BEST PHOTOGRAPHERS


Henri Cartir-Bresson




CLICK TO VISIT BIOGRAPHY

He is considered one of the best photographers that exists today, being on the hot spot for so long that he is considered the father of the photo report. This is due to photos he took on a trip he made through Europe and Asia, Henri was motivated to capture emotional, human images, which he captured at the right time.




Ansel Adams




CLICK TO VISIT BIOGRAPHY

This photography artist is characterized by having photographed landscapes, with many textures and realism, his photos are an expression of social reality.




Sebastiao Salgado




CLICK TO VISIT BIOGRAPHY

He is identified by making black and white images, the photos it takes constitute an expression of social reality. This artist born in 1944 in Aimorés, Brazil, has carried out several works throughout his career and got numerous awards and recognitions, his technique is inspired by the work of Robert Capa and Henri Cartier.




Bill Brandt




CLICK TO VISIT BIOGRAPHY

His style belongs to the surrealist movement, the images made by this artist show social reality in British society. Born in London in 1904, he studied painting and photography, in 1930 he worked as a freelance photographer, in 1940 he worked for different magazines and newspapers and in 1950 he consolidated taking photos of the English landscape




Julia Margaret




CLICK TO VISIT BIOGRAPHY

An English photography artist, her work was inspired by romantic painters in the religious field. She was born in the year 1815 in Garden Reach, was a descendant of French aristocrats, studied in Paris and England.
